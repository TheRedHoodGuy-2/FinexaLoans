FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can I get approved?

Most applications are reviewed within 2-24 hours. Once approved, funds are typically disbursed the same day or next business day.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid government ID (NIN or BVN), proof of income (bank statements or pay slips), and proof of address. Everything can be uploaded digitally.

What's the minimum and maximum loan amount?

Loan amounts range from ₦50,000 to ₦200,000,000 depending on the loan type and your eligibility. Personal loans go up to ₦5M while mortgages can reach ₦200M.

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not. We believe in full transparency. The interest rate you see is what you pay. No processing fees, no hidden charges.

Can I repay my loan early?

Yes! You can make early repayments at any time with no penalties. We actually encourage it and offer a slight discount on interest for early payoff.
